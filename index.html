How to play

Welcome Avery Buehler's "Word Guesser" game!

The purpose of this game is to complete the word seen on screen before the time runs out.

There are three difficulty levels. As you get more answers correct, the game will become harder.

Compete against yourself or friends on the Local Leaderboard!
